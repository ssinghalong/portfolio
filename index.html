Smart Stick for the Blind

Using Arduino, GSM module, GPS module, soil moisture sensor, and Ultrasonic sensor prepared a stick which when used by a blind person could detect obstacles in front, could detect any pools of water, and most importantly sent the location (URL which can be opened using Google maps) of the user on the phone-number of his/her well wishers with just the press of a button.

RFID Based Smart Shelf

The proposed system is designed such that each item is equipped with its own cheap RFID chip which when put in a basket also equipped with one can be added, this results in the reduction of the hassle of standing and waiting in long queues by billing from the cart itself. Another benefit is security against potential theft as and replenishing the stock.
